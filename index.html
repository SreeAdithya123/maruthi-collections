A Kalamkari boutique in Nidadavole, Andhra Pradesh — curated by Sai Priyanka Godavari since 2018. Hand-block half-&-half kurtis, frocks for women and girls, cotton sarees and easy daily-wear drapes, chosen one piece at a time.

Maruthi Collections is a Kalamkari boutique in Nidadavole, West Godavari, Andhra Pradesh, established 2018 and curated by Sai Priyanka Godavari. We specialise in hand-block Kalamkari: half-&-half Kalamkari cotton kurtis (women's sizes S to XXL), Kalamkari frocks for women and little girls (kids age sizes 2-3Y to 12-13Y), Kalamkari cotton sarees including Srikalahasti pen-Kalamkari and Machilipatnam block-print, and soft daily-wear cotton sarees from under five hundred rupees. Everything is natural-dyed cotton — myrobalan, madder and indigo — at honest, everyday prices. The brand voice is warm, heritage, editorial and unhurried — a quiet boutique, not a mass marketplace. Brand colour palette: warm ivory (#F5EFE3), deep maroon (#6B1F25 / #4A1418), antique gold (#B8895A / #D4AC7E), indigo (#26304F) and Kalamkari teal (#1F4D44). Typography: Cormorant Garamond, Cinzel and Lato. Visit the boutique in Nidadavole or call Sai Priyanka at +91 86392 32932. Tagline: Threads of the Godavari.
