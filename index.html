A boutique handloom saree house in Nidadavole, Andhra Pradesh — curated by Sai Priyanka Godavari since 2018. Kanjivaram, Banarasi, Pochampally, Uppada Pattu, Gadwal and Venkatagiri, hand-picked one weave at a time.

Maruthi Collections is a boutique saree house in Nidadavole, West Godavari, Andhra Pradesh, established 2018 and curated by Sai Priyanka Godavari. We offer GI-certified heritage handloom weaves: Kanjivaram from Kanchipuram, Banarasi from Varanasi, Pochampally double ikat, Gadwal korvai silk-cotton, Uppada Pattu jamdani, Venkatagiri and Mangalagiri fine cotton. Sarees for bridal, festive, office and daily wear in pure mulberry silk, katan silk, silk-cotton and fine cotton. The brand voice is warm, heritage, editorial and unhurried — a quiet boutique, not a mass marketplace. Brand colour palette: warm ivory (#F5EFE3), deep maroon (#6B1F25 / #4A1418), antique zari gold (#B8895A / #D4AC7E), peacock blue (#2A4A6B) and peacock teal (#2D6B5F). Typography: Cormorant Garamond, Cinzel and Lato. Visit the boutique in Nidadavole or call Sai Priyanka at +91 86392 32932. Tagline: Threads of the Godavari.
