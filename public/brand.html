Maruthi Collections

Threads of the Godavari

A boutique handloom saree house in Nidadavole, West Godavari, Andhra Pradesh — curated personally by Sai Priyanka Godavari since 2018. Every weave is hand-picked, one at a time. Curated handloom sarees from across South India, treated as heirlooms, not inventory.

The Curator

Sai Priyanka Godavari grew up watching her mother fold sarees the way other mothers folded prayers. Maruthi Collections is the quiet boutique she imagined — where weavers' work is treated as art. Every saree is chosen by Priyanka herself, from Kanchipuram looms, Uppada weavers and Pochampally artisans she has known for years. No bulk orders, no mass imports.

Voice & tone

Warm, heritage, editorial and unhurried — a quiet boutique, not a marketplace. We speak as a curator, not a salesperson. We use the language of the loom (kongu, korvai, buti, zari, jamdani, pallu) and never hard-sell. No "BUY NOW", no "FLASH SALE". The Godavari, the weaver, and the woman who chooses each saree are at the centre of every story.
